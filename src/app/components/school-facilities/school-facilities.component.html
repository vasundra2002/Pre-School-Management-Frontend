School Facilities

A childcare centre might offer a different type of early childhood education from kindergarten, but both of them are under the category of preschool. Either kindergarten, childcare centre or any preschool, children spent lots of learning times and playtimes at there. As a learning environment, space must be RESPONSIVE to the needs of children as well as to the FLEXIBILITY of the educators. Have you ever thought about the role of the facilities in preschool play in early childhood education? Do you know there are some must-have facilities in preschool that can help to build a better learning environment? These five preschool facilities have proven their value in enhancing a better quality of learning environment. Let’s check them out!

School Bus

"Our Wheels Roll for Learning," "Safety First: Kids Ride with Us," and "School Buses are the Safest Way to Roll."

Playground

Learning Through Play... You can’t have a playground without play. To put it simply, play is a spontaneous activity children engage in to have fun.

Healthy Canteen

Don't let hunger strike, come to the school canteen tonight. Fill your tummy with yumminess, right here in our school canteen.

Positive Learning

Attitude Is A Little Thing That Makes A Big Difference Learning Today . . . Leading Tomorrow We teach, model, and encourage a love of learning, collaboration, and compassion for others.
