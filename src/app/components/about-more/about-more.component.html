In general, children can go to preschool when they’re 4 years old, or in the year they turn 4. Some preschools in some states and territories also offer programs for 3-year-olds.

Sometimes things can get in the way of children going to preschool. Play is a great way to support your child’s learning and development at home. You can encourage your child to try play activities that are like the activities they’d do at preschool – for example, playing with cardboard boxes, making a collage and reading together.
